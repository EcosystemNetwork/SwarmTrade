Welcome to Swarm Trade

Autonomous multi-agent trading platform

Your swarm of AI agents works together to find opportunities, assess risk, and execute trades automatically. Here's how to read the dashboard:

1
Market Data & Price Chart
The center shows live price action with candlestick charts. Your P&L and portfolio allocation are on the right.
2
AI Agents (left sidebar)
38 specialized agents — scouts find data, analysts generate signals, risk agents approve/reject, and executors fill trades. Green dot = active now.
3
Signals (right sidebar, top)
Agents produce buy/sell signals with strength and confidence. The composite score combines them — green bar right = buy pressure, red bar left = sell pressure.
4
Risk Review (right sidebar, bottom)
Every trade must pass a committee of risk agents. Click any trade in the table to see each agent's vote. 3+ approvals = consensus.
5
Pending Trades & Trade History (center, bottom)
Watch trades flow from pending → approved → filled. Switch tabs to see execution history with fill prices, slippage, and P&L per trade.
