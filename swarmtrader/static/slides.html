Intelligent Strategy

Regime-Aware Weighting

Automatic weight profiles based on detected market regime:

Trending Momentum 2x | MeanRev 0.3x
Mean Reverting MeanRev 2.5x | Momentum 0.3x
Volatile PRISM 1.5x | Funding 1.5x

Adaptive Learning

Weights evolve over time: agents whose signals align with realized PnL get +5% boost, misaligned agents get -3%. No retraining needed.

Kelly Criterion Sizing

Mathematically optimal position sizing:

f* = (p * b - q) / b
p = win rate | b = payoff ratio | q = 1 - p
Uses half-Kelly for safety (clamped 5%..50%)

8 Layers of Risk Management

Every trade passes through unanimous multi-agent risk consensus

groups

Risk Quorum

4 independent risk agents must ALL approve. One veto = trade blocked.

emergency

Circuit Breaker

Halts after 5 consecutive losses, max drawdown breach, or volatility spike.

speed

Rate Limiter

Max 20 trades/hour. Prevents overtrading in choppy markets.

trending_down

Drawdown Limit

Configurable daily max loss with automatic midnight UTC reset.

power_settings_new

Kill Switch

Instant halt: touch KILL or one-click from web dashboard.

sync_problem

Dead Man's Switch

Auto-cancels ALL open orders via Kraken CLI if agent becomes unresponsive.

compress

Position Flattener

Emergency close of ALL positions when circuit breaker fires.

receipt_long

Full Audit Trail

Every intent, verdict, and execution logged to SQLite. Full PnL attribution per agent.
